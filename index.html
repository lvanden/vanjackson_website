02. Projects

Data Analysis of Women's Professional Soccer


With the hope of growing the game, I wanted to create more conversation around our women’s professional soccer league (NWSL) here in the U.S. Having a background in computer science and my friend’s in data science we worked together to analyze and predict how teams will do this season. With much positive feedback, I created a Slack group for women and other underrepresented genders around the women’s game and data analysis. This is a space where deeper conversations are happening and more folks are learning, programming, and connecting in ways I never imagined!
